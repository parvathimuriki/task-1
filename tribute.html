Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

"The Missile Man of India"

Why I Admire Him

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was not just a brilliant scientist but also a humble human being who inspired millions of people with his vision, simplicity, and dedication to the nation. His journey from a small town in Rameswaram to becoming the President of India shows that hard work and determination can help one achieve great heights.

I admire his deep love for students and his belief that the youth of a country are its true strength. His speeches and writings continue to motivate me to dream big, work hard, and serve society selflessly.

Famous Quote

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
